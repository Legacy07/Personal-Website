Interview Revision

Overview

An Android Application mainly for graduates and students who want to revise and have a look at what sorts of questions may be asked generally and in specific IT-related job interviews. Also typical answers are provided to give you a head-start. The application provides the following features;

  • General Interview-Related Questions and ideal Answers
  • Tips on Face-to-Face & Telephone Interview, also Group Discussion and CV Tips
  • Technical Questions & Answers; Java and Android are included.
  • Algorithms; Data Structures, Searching, Sorting and Graph Algorithms.

Technologies

  • JAVA
  • XML
