Muscle Crate Review

Posted on 30/08/2019

In this day and age, it’s pretty much very possible to find a loot box in every category. So, this is no different and the idea is to send out selected products every month to monthly subscribers. In this case, Muscle Crate boxes contain a kg of protein powder and all kinds of products from protein bars to creatine samples etc. Obviously, there are different subscription boxes but I’m only reviewing what I’m receiving.

With this subscription box, you can choose either ‘Chocolate Type’ or ‘Fruit Type’ for protein powder flavors, so because I got fed up with chocolate flavor and desired to try out different kinds of flavors, I chose ‘Fruit Type’ and kept that selection for couple of months now. This month, I received a kg tub of protein powder which was full ‘vegan’, didn’t really looked up for its price but vegan products are quite pricey normally. Also, the flavor was ‘Cinnamon Churro’, I was disgusted when I read cinnamon on the label however, I still wanted to give a try and see how it goes, surprisingly enough I quite liked its ‘rich flavor’ and thickness. Besides that, I received couple of other samples ranging from protein powders, creatine, pre-workout, bcaa and aminos. Also, I received a packet of protein cookies that costs £4 on their site. In total I normally receive 8-12 different products including samples and products. One time I received protein Nutella and some other month received 250g of protein powder. Every month I also find these 500ml of pre-workout drinks too.

I always wanted this sort of loot boxing in this category, it’s nice to try out different products, brands and flavors. I believe it very much is worth the money as they really send out premium quality protein powders that is at least £18 and above and with all the other samples and products, it’s well hitting the £30 mark and most probably above as well.

If you want to check it out too, you can find it from here.
